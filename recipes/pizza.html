Pizza

Description

Pizza is quite possibly the greatest food on the planet. Consisting of carbs (dough), vegetables (sauce), protein (cheese, potential meats), it can and should be consumed at every time of day for a balanced diet.


Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Red sauce
  • Pepperoni (if you're cool)

Instructions

  1. Flatten the dough until it's round. Square pizzas will not be allowed in this lesson.
  2. Add the sauce evenly.
  3. Add the cheese judiciously.
  4. Put in a big oven until it looks like a cooked pizza.
  5. Enjoy!
